FAQ

Common Questions

How do I book an appointment?
+
All bookings are strictly by appointment. Send a DM on Instagram (@lacedbymide_) or message via WhatsApp (+234 906 468 2202). A deposit is required to secure your slot.
Do I need to bring my own hair?
+
Yes, for wig construction and installation services, you will need to bring your bundles and lace. Mide will advise you on the right amount and type of hair needed when you book.
How does the wig revamping drop-off work?
+
For revamping, you drop off your wig and it will be ready within the agreed timeframe. Drop-off location will be confirmed when you book via DM or WhatsApp.
Is a deposit required to book?
+
Yes, a non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment slot. The deposit amount will be communicated when you reach out to book.
How long does a full wig construction take?
+
Construction timelines vary depending on the complexity of the unit. Typically 2–5 business days. Exact timelines will be discussed during your booking consultation.
Do you offer bridal packages?
+
Yes! Bridal and special event packages are available. Enquire via WhatsApp or DM for custom pricing based on your event requirements. Early booking is strongly advised.
